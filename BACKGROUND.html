MANDATE

Uphold the People’s Constitutional Rights to Life and Property, By Addressing the Root Causes of Vulnerabilities to Disasters, Strengthening the Institutional Capacity for DRRM and Building the Resilience of Community to Disasters, Including Climate Change Impacts.

VISION

Mambuleños ensured with the right to life and property in 2040

MISSION

To establish a community with knowledge, skills, and awareness, prepared in meeting emergencies and climate change impacts, reducing its risk, responding effectively to save life, property, and balance the environment through a capable Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council and Climate Change Adaptation Council, Partners, and Volunteers. All taking action to attain maximum security.

ORGANIZATIONAL OUTCOME

Effective Delivery of Disaster Risk Reduction and Management services in coordination with the different sectors and agencies

SECTIONS AND FUNCTIONS

Functions:

a.) Responsible for the day to day operation of the LDRRM Office;

b.) Designs, programs and coordinates DRRM activities;

c.) Facilitates and supports risk assessment and planning activities at the local level;

d.) Consolidates local disaster risk information which includes natural hazards, vulnerabilities, and climate change risks, and maintains local risk maps;

e.) Conducts research and development initiatives on DRRM;

f.) Formulates and implements LDRRMP in close coordination with the MDRRMC;

g.) Prepares and submits to the Local Sanggunian through the LDRRMC and LDC the different plans and budget;

h.) Reports on LDRRM fund utilization, and other reports to COA, OCD, and DILG;

i.) Serves as the secretariat and executive arm of the Local DRMM council (LDRRMC);

j.) Ensures the involvement of the most vulnerable sectors in risk assessment planning;

k.) Supervises and monitors the BDRRM fund utilization and implementation of BDRRM Programs, Projects, and Activities (PPA’s);

l.) Implements policies, approved plans, and programs of the LDRRMC consistent with the policies and guidelines laid down in this Act;

m.) Establishes Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management operation center;

n.) Prepares and submits, through the LDRRMC and the LDC, the report on the utilization of the LDRRMF and other dedicated disaster risks reduction and management resources of the local COA, copy furnished the regional director of the OCD and the Local Government Operations Officer of the DILG;

o.) Acts on matters that may be authorized by the LDRRMC.

Functions:

a.) Organizes and conducts training, orientation, and knowledge management activities on DRRM at the local level.

b.) Identifies, assesses, and manages the hazards, vulnerabilities, and risks that may impact the community.

c.) Assists in the preparation of the Local DRRM Plan.

d.) Coordinates with other agencies for disaster response and mitigation activities.

Functions:

a.) Conducts research and risk assessments to provide data for DRRM planning.

b.) Prepares reports on disaster risk and vulnerability assessments.

c.) Recommends programs to strengthen DRRM capabilities in the community.
